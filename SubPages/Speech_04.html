学术报告(Wet process innovation of single crystalsilicon for 3-D structuring)


单 位：机电工程学院 
时间: 2012年7月6日上午10:00-11:30  
地点：工学二号馆205会议室
题目: Wet process innovation of single crystal silicon for 3-D structuring
主讲人: Professor Kazuo SATO（日本爱知工业大学教授，日本名古屋大学名义教授）

佐藤一雄教授简历：
He received BS degree from Yokohama National University in 1970, and PhD degree from The University of Tokyo in 1982. He worked with Hitachi Ltd. during

1970-1994. He was a professor of Micromachining and MEMS Laboratory, Nagoya University during 1994-2012. He became an Emeritus Professor of Nagoya

University. Since 2012, he is a professor at Aichi Institute of Technology.

He started MEMS research in 1983, and published 136 journal papers, 32 review articles, and 16 book chapters on MEMS. His research areas are micro/nano-

physics in anisotropic etching and mechanical properties of single crystal silicon, as well as applied microsystems such as sensors and actuators.


He co-chaired IEEE MEMS-97. He is the Editor in Asia of the Journal Micromechanics and Microengineering (IOP). He is a Fellow of JSME and JSPE, a Senior

Member of IEEJ, and a member of IEEE.

演讲主题:


Wet anisotropic etching of single crystal silicon has been used in MEMS fabrication. Conventional anisotropic etching used to be applied for making quite a limited

variation in shapes of microstructures such as square recess, square diaphragms and straight cantilevers. Due to recent progress in our wet process research, a

varieties of etch profiles became available by appropriate tuning of etching solution. Much more complicated 3-D structures can be fabricated including smooth

circular etch profiles on Si (100) wafers.
