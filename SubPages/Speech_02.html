学术报告(高频传感器及阵列的压电厚膜)

单 位：机电工程学院 
题目：高频传感器及阵列的压电厚膜
报告人： 周岐发 教授
报告时间：2012年5月24日（星期四）上午10:00-11:30
地点：工学2号馆205

    周歧发教授简历：1993年毕业于西安交通大学电子材料系，获博士学位，现为南加州大学Yiterbi工程学院生物医学系教授。在南加州大学工作之前曾经在中山大学物理系、香港理工大学应用物理系、宾州大学材料研究所进行铁电材料及器件的相关研究和开发工作。自2007年起为IEEE超声波、铁电及频率控制协会（UFFC-S）的高级会员，IEEE UFFC铁电委员会委员；2008年起为IEEE UFFC国际超声会议和SPIE超声成像的大会组委，此外，他还是IEEE TUFFC杂志的副主编。现在主要的研究方向为铁电薄/厚膜、压电单晶、无铅压电陶瓷、MEMS技术、生物医学压电传感器及阵列的制造技术、超声成像技术等。在铁电薄膜、陶瓷、器件及应用等领域发表论文120多篇。

报告简介：
     Piezoelectric Thick Films for High Frequency Transducer/Array Qifa ZhouNIH Transducer Resource Center and Department of Biomedical Engineering,University of Southern California, Los Angeles, CA 90089High frequency (>30 MHz) ultrasonic imaging has been extensively used for imaging of eye, blood vessel, skin and small animals and intravacular. Fabrication of the transducer, which is the most critical component of the ultrasound imaging system, becomes challenging especially when very high frequency (>100 MHz) is required. Conventional lapping-and-dicing of bulk piezoelectric materails will no longer be a viable option. Piezoelectric thick films may be a good alternative for fabricating high frequency ultrasound transducers using MEMS technology. This talk presents the latest development of high-frequency ultrasonic transducer and arrays with high-quality PZT thick films. PZT thick films in thickness of greater than 10 µm were successfully prepared with a spin-coating of PZT composite sol-gel solution. Film quality was enchanced by infilitration of PZT sol-gel into composite films and optimization of PZT powder to PZT sol-gel mass ratio in composite solution. Single element transducers and linear arrays at frequencies higher than 100 MHz were fabricated with the PZT thick films. To fabricate the kerfless array, a layer of Au was patterned as the top electrodes via photolithography. A conductive epoxy, E-solder 3022, was used as a backing material after the backside silicon was removed with KOH solution. One major problem with the kerfless arrays is their large crosstalk. To decrease the crosstalk, the array elements have to be seperated. Inductively coupled plasma-Reactive ion etching (ICP-RIE) based dry etching was selected to etch the PZT thick films into kerfed arrays. These PZT thick films were found to have a remanent polarization of 33 µC/cm2 and a coercive field Ec of 70 kV/cm. The 36-elements linear array showed a -6dB bandwidth of 60% and an insertion loss of around -40 dB with a center frequency of 120 MHz, respectively. In addition, results on lead-free films, ceramics and single crystal for medical imaging application will also be presented and discussed.
