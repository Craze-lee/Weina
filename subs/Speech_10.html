学术报告(Research on hybrid multifunctional hard coatings)

单 位：机电工程学院 
报告题目：Research on hybrid multifunctional hard coatings
报告人： Prof. Kwang-ho Kim
报告时间: 2013年1月9日. 上午10:00~11:00.
报告地点: 大学城工学2号馆730室.
Biography of Prof. Kim: 
      Prof. Kwangho Kim, received his PhD’s degree in Korean Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in 1986 and became a professor of Pusan National University from 1985. He is now the director of National Core Research Center for Hybrid Materials Solution, director of National Research Laboratory (thin film division), vice president of the Korean Institute of Ceramic Society, non-executive director of Korean Research Foundation, editorial board of Korean Union of Chemical Science and Technology Society, etc. His current research is focused on designing and investigating hybrid materials through multi-scale hybridization (macro scale, micro scale, atomic scale, crystalline/amorphous, inter- and intra- granular, etc.).

ABSTRACT:
     Hard coatings of multi-functional nanocomposite for the structural application are introduced in this presentation. Design and syntheses of those coatings based on not only superhardness but also low friction coefficient were conducted for the multi-component materials system. For example, various ternary, quaternary and quinary nano-structured coatings in the (Ti, Cr, Mo, Al, Si)-(B, C, N) system are compared in sense of superhardness as well as properties of corrosion, oxidation, friction etc. MeN/soft Me (Me = metal) system, e.g., MoN/Cu film were examined for low friction application. Codeposition nitride/carbide of transition metals together with the SiNx, SiC, BN, or soft metals resulted in formation of nanocomposite coatings and led to superhardness, high thermal stability, and low-friction coefficients, etc.
